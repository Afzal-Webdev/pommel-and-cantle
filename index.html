Our Story

Born from the Saddle,
Crafted with Passion.

For over 10 years being in the equestrian world from early morning rides to competitive events, horses have been our constant companions teaching us the importance of trust and the perfect partnership between rider and tack.

That deep connection inspired us to channel our passion into the art of leather craftsmanship. What started as a desire for better, more reliable gear evolved into a mission to supply premium saddles and high-quality leather rolls that stand the test of time.

Every saddle we offer is selected (or crafted) with the rider's comfort and the horse's well-being in mind—drawing directly from our years in the saddle. Our leather rolls are sourced for superior quality, ideal for custom projects or repairs, ensuring you get materials that age beautifully and perform flawlessly whether for your furniture, shoes, car or jacket.
