Our Story

Leather Craftsmanship
Since 1847

For over 175 years, Pommel & Cantle has been synonymous with uncompromising quality and timeless craftsmanship. What began in a small workshop in the English countryside has grown into a legacy trusted by equestrians and artisans worldwide.

Every piece we create tells a story—of skilled hands, premium materials, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Our master craftsmen use techniques passed down through generations, combined with modern precision, to create leather goods that aren't just products, but heirlooms.

We believe in slow craft, sustainable practices, and the kind of quality that improves with age. Each saddle, sheet, and accessory is meticulously inspected to meet our exacting standards before it earns the Pommel & Cantle name.
